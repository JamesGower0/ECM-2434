Welcome to the ExeterNest Homepage

Engaging and educational mobile game designed to raise awareness about sustainability at the University of Exeter. Players explore different areas on Streatham campus, solve challenges, and answer quiz questions related to sustainability practices at the university. The app aims to make learning fun and accessible, encouraging players to adopt eco-friendly practices in their daily lives. Persuasive gamification is used in the form of quizzes, this will give users a score which will reward them for being well-informed on sustainability in the context of the University.
