Welcome to the ExeterNest Homepage

Engaging and educational mobile game designed to raise awareness about sustainability at the University of Exeter.
Players explore different areas on Streatham campus, solve challenges, and answer quiz questions related to sustainability practices at the university.
The app aims to make learning fun and accessible, encouraging players to adopt eco-friendly practices in their daily lives.
Persuasive gamification is used in the form of quizzes, this will give users a score which will reward them for being well-informed on sustainability in the context of the University.

You can access all of the pages from the navigation panel but make sure you are registered to do that!
You will have your own profile with your Bird.
Click the shop to see the accessories that you can buy for your NestAvatar.
If you are not happy with your Bird, feel free to buy another one in the shop!
How can you afford those items? Answer quiz questions and solve the challenges to get the NestPoints!

You will notice that your bird has 'Mood'.
This will decrease every hour in the afternoon, so make sure you answer the quiz questions every day for your Bird to stay happy!
In case you Bird's mood is at 0, your NestPoints will start to decrease, so make sure you play daily!
